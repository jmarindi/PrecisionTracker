Privacy Policy

Data Collection

PrecisionTracker collects zero personal data. The app does not transmit any information to external servers. All GPS coordinates, step counts, session records, and route photos are stored exclusively in your device's local storage.

Location Permissions

The app requests access to your device's precise location solely to record your movement route during active sessions. Location data is never shared, uploaded, or used for any purpose other than generating your local fitness record.

Camera & Storage

Camera access is only requested when you choose to take a route photo within an active session. Photos are saved directly to your device and are never uploaded. Storage access is used solely to write and read your session files.

Data Deletion

You can permanently delete all your stored sessions and data at any time from within the app's settings screen. Uninstalling the app also removes all locally stored data.
