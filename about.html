Your Privacy, Guaranteed

A completely local experience — no exceptions

In an era of constant cloud synchronization, PrecisionTracker stands apart by offering a completely local experience:

No Cloud Storage

We do not use external servers. Your GPS coordinates, photos, and fitness stats reside strictly on your phone.

Zero Data Sharing

Your data is never shared with third parties. You have total control over when and where you export your information.

Technical Requirements & Performance

What your device needs for best results

To ensure the highest level of accuracy and functionality, PrecisionTracker requires the following:

GPS / Location Services — Must be enabled and set to "High Accuracy" for precise route tracing.
Permissions — Location (tracking), Camera (route photos), and Storage (exporting data files).
Motion Sensors — Accelerometer access required for integrated step-counter functionality.
Android 8.0 (Oreo) or higher — Required for full feature compatibility.
